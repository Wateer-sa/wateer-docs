# Introduction

Welcome to Wateer, Saudi Arabia's leading digital receipt platform.

## Overview

Wateer transforms how businesses and consumers manage receipts. By digitizing the entire receipt lifecycle, we reduce paper waste, improve record-keeping, and enhance the customer experience.

## Why Wateer?

- **For Businesses:** Reduce costs, improve efficiency, stay compliant
- **For Customers:** Never lose a receipt, track expenses automatically
- **For the Environment:** Reduce paper waste across the Kingdom
